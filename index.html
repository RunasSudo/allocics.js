allocics.js

One-click export of Allocate+ timetables to ICS format for Google Calendar, iCalendar, etc.

Let's do it!

  1. Click and drag this link – Export Timetable – to your bookmarks bar.
  2. Log in to Allocate+, and go to the ‘Timetable’ tab.
  3. Click the bookmark!
  4. That's it! Just download the ICS file and import it into your calendar.
