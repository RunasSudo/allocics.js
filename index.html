IMPROVED One-click export of Allocate+ timetables to ICS format for Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, etc.

Let's do it!

  1. Click and drag this link – Export Timetable – to your bookmarks bar.
  2. Log in to Allocate+.
  3. Click the bookmark!
  4. That's it! Just download the ICS file and import it into your calendar.

Google Calendar

  1. Log in to Google Calendar.
  2. Click the cog button at the top right-hand corner, then click ‘Settings’.
  3. Go to the ‘Calendars’ tab, then click ‘Import calendar’.
  4. Select the ICS file you downloaded and follow the instructions to finish the import.

Apple Calendar

  1. Open the Calendar app.
  2. From the menu, choose ‘File’, then ‘Import’.
  3. Select the ICS file you downloaded and follow the instructions to finish the import.
